In English:

Who is a Doctor?
A doctor is a qualified professional who diagnoses and treats illnesses, prescribes medications, and helps people live healthier lives.

How to Become a Doctor?

  • Complete 12th with Biology, Physics, Chemistry.
  • Write NEET (National Eligibility Entrance Test).
  • Get admission in MBBS based on NEET score.
  • MBBS duration is 5.5 years (including internship).
  • After MBBS, pursue specialization (MD/MS) for higher roles.

Types of Doctors:

  • General Physician
  • Surgeon (MS)
  • Pediatrician (Child Doctor)
  • Cardiologist (Heart)
  • Dermatologist (Skin)
  • Gynecologist (Women's Health)
  • Orthopedic (Bones & Joints)
  • ENT Specialist (Ear, Nose, Throat)
  • Psychiatrist (Mental Health)

Work Areas:

  • Hospitals (Govt. & Private)
  • Clinics
  • Medical Colleges
  • Research & Pharma
  • Army / Navy / Railways

Other Medical Degrees:

  • BDS тАУ Dentist
  • BAMS тАУ Ayurveda
  • BHMS тАУ Homeopathy
  • BUMS тАУ Unani
  • BVSc тАУ Veterinary Doctor
