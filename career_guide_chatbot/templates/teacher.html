Who is a Teacher?
A teacher is a professional who helps students gain knowledge, values, and skills. They work in schools, colleges, coaching centers, or online platforms.

  • тЬФя╕П Complete 12th standard (any group)
  • тЬФя╕П Pursue a bachelor's degree (B.A, B.Sc, B.Com)
  • тЬФя╕П Then do B.Ed (Bachelor of Education)
  • тЬФя╕П Clear TET (Teacher Eligibility Test тАУ TNTET / CTET)
  • тЬФя╕П Apply for teaching jobs in private or government schools

Alternative Path (Pre-Primary):

  • тЬФя╕П After 12th, do D.El.Ed (Diploma in Elementary Education)
  • тЬФя╕П Eligible to teach from LKG to 5th standard

Career Roles in Teaching Field:

  • Primary, High School & Higher Secondary Teacher
  • College Lecturer (via PG + NET/SET)
  • Special Education Teacher
  • Online / Digital Educator

Duties of a Teacher:

  • Prepare lessons and teach subjects clearly
  • Assess students and guide them
  • Handle exams, reports and classroom discipline
  • Encourage creativity and critical thinking
