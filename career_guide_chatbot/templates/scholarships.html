ЁЯОБ Scholarships for Students

In English:

  • ЁЯОУ First Graduate Scholarship тАУ for students who are the first in their family to pursue college.
  • ЁЯОУ Government Merit Scholarship тАУ for students with high marks in 10th or 12th.
  • ЁЯОУ SC/ST Scholarship тАУ for students from SC/ST categories with income below the limit.
  • ЁЯОУ BC/MBC/DNC Scholarship тАУ available for Backward Class students.
  • ЁЯОУ Private Scholarships тАУ like Reliance Foundation, Sitaram Jindal, Tata Trusts, etc.
  • ЁЯОУ National Scholarship Portal (NSP) тАУ One place for all central + state scholarships (scholarships.gov.in)
