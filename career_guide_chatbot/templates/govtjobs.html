What are Government Jobs?

Government jobs offer stable careers, job security, pension, and benefits. They are categorized as:

  • Group A: IAS, IPS, IFS, IRS (UPSC)
  • Group B: State-level Officers (TNPSC Group 1, 2)
  • Group C: Clerks, Assistants (SSC, TNPSC Group 4, RRB)
  • Group D: Peons, Technicians, Drivers, MTS

Popular Government Exams:

  • UPSC тАУ IAS, IPS, IFS, IRS
  • TNPSC тАУ Group 1, 2, 4
  • SSC тАУ CGL, CHSL, MTS
  • RRB тАУ Railway Jobs
  • Bank тАУ IBPS, SBI PO, Clerk
  • Police тАУ TNUSRB Constable, SI
  • Post Office, TET, Army, Navy, etc.
