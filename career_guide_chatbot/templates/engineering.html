ЁЯФ╣ What is an Engineering Degree?

An engineering degree is a professional qualification that teaches students to design, develop, and maintain technologies, machines, and systems. Most courses are B.E. or B.Tech, 4 years after 12th standard (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics).

Engineering offers vast career opportunities in India and abroad.
